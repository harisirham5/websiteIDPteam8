Welcome to readlens

Malaysia faces a growing accessibility challenge: over 300,000 citizens live with severe visual impairment, and myopia affects nearly one third of the population.

readlens directly addresses this gap by providing an affordable, AI-powered smart glasses system for blind and myopic users, combining real-time obstacle detection, text recognition, and audio feedback.
