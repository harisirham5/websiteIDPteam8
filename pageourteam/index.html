Meet the Team

The innovators behind readlens.

Hariz

Project Lead & System Integrator

Oversees the entire development process, ensuring all hardware and software components work seamlessly together. Responsible for project planning, documentation, and aligning the design with user needs and accessibility goals.

Irham

AI & Software Developer

Designs and implements the readlens smartphone app and AI chatbot. Handles image processing for OCR and object detection, Bluetooth communication with the ESP32-S3, and ensures smooth user interaction through the app interface.

Adam

Hardware & Circuit Designer

Develops the electronic architecture of the smart glasses, including the ESP32-S3 microcontroller, OV5640 camera, distance sensor, TP4056 charging module, AMS1117-3.3V regulator, and Li-Po battery integration. Focuses on power efficiency and compact design.

Amir

Audio & User Experience Engineer

Optimizes the audio feedback system using micro earphone speaker drivers. Ensures clear, low-latency sound output and comfortable wearability for blind and myopic users. Works closely with the design team to refine ergonomics and usability.

Haziq

Testing & Validation Specialist

Conducts functional, performance, and user-based testing to validate OCR accuracy, obstacle detection reliability, and overall system responsiveness. Collects feedback from visually impaired users and refines the design for real-world application.
